About Kickride

Kickride Co. was born right here in the Bahamas to give locals and visitors a faster, greener way to get around. Whether you’re island‑hopping through Nassau’s colorful streets or simply need a quick, traffic‑free commute to work, our electric bikes and scooters are always nearby, always charged, and always ready to roll.

Island Explorers

Tourists love Kickride for on‑demand trips from the cruise port to Bay Street. No lines, no hidden fees. Just hop on, tap your phone, and enjoy every turquoise vista.

Daily Commuters

Skip the morning jam on Soldier Road. Our reliable e‑bikes zip you to work in minutes, with affordable hourly rates and docking stations across every neighborhood.

Family Adventures

Planning a beach day at Cable Beach? Bring the kids along on our tandem scooters or cargo e‑bikes, complete with helmets and safety locks. Fun for everyone, big or small!
