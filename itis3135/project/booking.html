Get Started

Booking in 3 Simple Steps

1

Pick Your Ride

Open the Kickride app and tap on the nearest available bike or scooter.

2

Scan & Pay

Scan the QR code on the handlebar, choose pay‑as‑you‑go or a pass, and unlock.

3

Ride & Return

Enjoy your trip. Park at any public station and tap “End Ride” to lock up.
