Our Services

At Kickride, we make getting around the Bahamas simple, eco‑friendly, and fun. Whether you’re a local commuter, a day tripping tourist, or planning a big event, we have a plan to fit your needs. Each unlocking our fleet of electric bikes and scooters via the Kickride app, 24/7 roadside assistance, and basic accident protection.

Pay‑As‑You‑Go

No commitment. Just unlock, ride, and pay for the minutes you use.

  • $0.15/minute (billed per second)
  • No subscription or monthly fees
  • Unlimited trips up to 2 hours each
  • Basic accident protection included

Tourist Day Pass

Perfect for vacationers exploring Nassau, Paradise Island, or beyond.

  • $20 for 24 hours of unlimited 45‑minute rides
  • Extra 15 free minutes per ride
  • Exclusive discounts at partner cafes and tours

Monthly Pass

Best value for frequent riders who commute or island‑hop often.

  • $25/month for unlimited 60‑minute rides
  • $0.10/minute thereafter
  • Priority in‑app support & bonus ride credits
  • Free helmet rental at downtown stations

Event & Festival Rentals

Make your wedding, retreat, or community festival stand out.

  • Flat‑rate packages for 5+ bikes/scooters
  • On‑site delivery, setup, and pick‑up
  • Safety briefing & ride coordinator included
  • Half‑day, full‑day, or multi‑day options
