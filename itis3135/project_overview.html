Kickride Project Overview

Website Plan

  • This website is being developed to promote Kickride, an electric bike‑sharing platform for the Bahamas.
  • Intended users:city commuters, tourists, and local businesses.
  • The site will include: Home, About Us, Services, How to Book Us, and Contact pages.

Wireframe

Site Map

Page Design

Home

  • Purpose: Main landing page and brand intro.
  • Audience: All visitors (locals & tourists).
  • Content: Welcome message and featured bike image
  • Interactivity: Image slideshow of a Kickride.

About Us

  • Purpose: Company background & mission.
  • Audience: Prospective riders & partners.
  • Content: Story of Kickride, user‑group cards (Tourists, Commuters, Families).

Services

  • Purpose: Explain rental plans & pricing.
  • Audience: Local commuters, day‑tripping tourists, event organizers.
  • Content: Pay‑As‑You‑Go, Day Pass, Monthly, Corporate & Event packages.

How to Book Us

  • Purpose: Guide users through booking.
  • Audience: Riders ready to reserve a bike or scooter.
  • Content: 3‑step process with images: Pick, Scan & Pay, Ride & Return.
  • Interactivity: Anchor link “Get Started” scrolls to steps.

Contact

  • Purpose: Inquiries & support.
  • Audience: Anyone with questions or feedback.
  • Content: Contact info, email/phone links, and a submission form.
  • Validation: Client‑side required fields & AJAX form submit.

Dynamic Functionality

  • Google Maps API – show station locations on an interactive map.
  • jQuery Validation – validate the contact form before submit.
  • jQuery UI Dialog – use a modal for “Reserve Now” on the booking page.
  • Lightbox2 – optional image lightbox on the Home page slideshow.
